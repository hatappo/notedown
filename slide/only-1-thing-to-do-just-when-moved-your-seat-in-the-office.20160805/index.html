Japan is narrow

  • The office spaces are also tight.
  • Changing the seating arrangements is more frequent events than other countries, e.g. U.S.

What is the most important thing when moving?

  • Don't forget anything?
  • Keep the things less?
  • Let your previous seat clear and clean?
  • Proceed with your work quickly?

I think that there are more important things.

That is...

To share a confidence with your new neighbors bright and early

Today, I introduce a tool support this situation.

command_history_wordcloud.py

command_history_wordcloud.py

merely just only generates a word cloud image

of your command line history.

  • After moved, let your neighbor to generate the word cloud image.
  • Only seeing that is also fun, because we rarely see the command usage of others.
  • In most cases you'l say that "too big `ls`!" because `ls` are predominantly big on anyone's shell in general.
  • And let your neighbor to explain your unknown command to you.
  • This may be great useful support for your work.
  • Although these tiny know-how are usually remain hidden in a individual.
  • These are more efficient for you if you moved to the new seat (especially as a newcomer for a team).

Usage

command_history_wordcloud needs python 3.

				pip install command_history_wordcloud
				chwc
